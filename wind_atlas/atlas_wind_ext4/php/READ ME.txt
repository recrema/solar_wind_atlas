The data is given as follows:
The files ending with:
br : Browse file
th : Thumbnail file.
R : Red Band
G : Green Band
B : Blue Band
N : Near-Infrared (NIR) Band
P : Panchromatic Band
PR : Pansharped Red Band
PG : Pansharped Green Band
PB : Pansharped Blue Band
PN : Pansharped NIR Band

Name Format
D2_<YYYYMMDDHHMMSS>_<OrbitNumber>_<OutputProductLevel>_<FileType>.tif
 
Spatial resolution of the Panchromatic and the pansharped bands are in 1m.
(P, PR, PG, PB, PN)
Spatial resolution of the multispectral bands are in 4m.
(R, G, B, N)
